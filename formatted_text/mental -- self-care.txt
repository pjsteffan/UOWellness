# Self-Care | Counseling Services

## Self-Care

Expanding your wellness skills toolkit and practicing holistic self-care can help you overcome setbacks and unexpected issues. If you're facing a challenge that feels too big to handle on your own, [reach out to us](https://counseling.uoregon.edu/services) for support.

---

### Common Concerns
College is a transitional time in life, and many students struggle. While everyone's experience is different, we've gathered information on a few challenges students often seek support for. We've also provided relevant advice and resources for each issue so you can get the help you need.  
**[Browse Issues](https://counseling.uoregon.edu/concerns)**

---

### Self-Care Kit
Whether you're working with a mental health professional or looking to improve your wellbeing on your own, there are many self-care strategies and techniques you can practice outside of therapy. This list of coping strategies is a great place to start.  
**[Explore Coping Strategies](https://counseling.uoregon.edu/copingstrategies)**
