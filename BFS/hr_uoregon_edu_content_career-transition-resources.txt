Career and Financial Counseling Confidential one-on-one sessions with a licensed counselor are available and can be customized for each individual based on their specific career transition needs. Areas of assistance can include a general assessment of interests, motivation, and job skills, writing a resume and cover letter, interviewing skills, job search assistance, and many other career transition services. Financial coaches are also available and can provide a needs analysis and an online written action plan to help develop better spending habits, reduce debt, improve credit, and increase savings to employees. EAP representatives can also help with the emotional challenges and adjustments related to a career transition. Schedule an Appointment: Contact Canopy directly to schedule an appointment by calling 800-433-2320 or texting 503-980-1770. Benefit eligible employees can contact Canopy to schedule a career counseling appointment at a time that is most convenient.Â  EAP services are available for up to 30 days after the last day of employment.
