Read additional information about the Parent PLUS. Apply for a PLUS Loan: Go to StudentAid.gov (secure site) Log in using parent FSA ID and password and apply If you are a first-time borrower or if you have had an endorser on a previous PLUS loan, complete a Master Promissory Note (MPN) Three term maximum Limited to financial aid eligibility Interest rate Fixed at 8.05% (for loans first disbursed on or after July 1, 2023, and before July 1, 2024) Interest rate Fixed at 9.08% (for loans first disbursed on or after July 1, 2024, and before July 1, 2025) Net fee per disbursement 4.228% (for loans first disbursed on or after October 1, 2020, and before October 1, 2024) Repayment begins 60 days after loan is fully disbursed (unless deferred) Apply Online Required Master Promissory Note (MPN) Required If Your PLUS Loan Request Is Denied There are three possible courses of action: Appeal the credit decision Call Direct Loans at 1-800-557-7394 Appeal online at StudentAid.gov Pursue the PLUS with an endorser (cosigner) Complete online at StudentAid.gov Student requests additional unsubsidized loan in lieu of the PLUS The parent and student complete the Ineligible Parent Borrower for Direct PLUS Loan form. Note: the maximum amount of additional unsubsidized loans is up to $4,000 per year for students with fewer than 90 earned credit hours, and up to $5,000 per year for students with 90 or more earned credit hours.
