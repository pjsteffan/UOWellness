Make the most of your experience at UO! VitaNavis is a tool provided by the UO that uses the SuperStrong assessment to match your personality and interests with educational and career pathways. Take the SuperStrong Assessment (about 10-15 minutes) and then you can review your results, save interests, and more! Requires Duck ID. Returning Users Already took the SuperStrong? Log in to VitaNavis anytime (click Sign in with University of Oregon) to review your results, identify academic options at the UO that support your career goals, further explore academic and career options, and/or retake the SuperStrong. Want to dive deeper? Connect with an advisor to review your results and get help connecting your interests and strengths with educational and career paths. Frequently Asked Questions I was able to log in to VitaNavis with my Duck ID, but I didn't land in the assessment; I just see the Dashboard. How can I get to the SuperStrong Assessment? New users must select Yes to connect to the University of Oregon. If they skip this step, they will land in the Dashboard and will not have any Activities. If this occurs, please send these instructions to the student: Log in to VitaNavis: https://vitanavis.com/uoregon Click Log In Click Sign in with University of Oregon Click on Have an Access Code? and enter this code: uoregon-2022 Still not working? Contact vitanavis@uoregon.edu Can I use VitaNavis on my phone? Yes! VitaNavis is mobile-friendly. You may find some of the results easier to view on a larger screen, such as a computer. Can I use VitaNavis after I leave the UO? Yes! However, you MUST update your email address in your UO VitaNavis profile to another email address before your UO Duck ID is no longer valid (in other words, before you finish at the UO). When you're ready to change your email in VitaNavis, click on your profile (top right), then go to Emails and change your Primary Email to your non-UO email address. Once you update your email to a non-UO email address, you will log in to http://www.vitanavis.com and then can continue to access your VitaNavis profile and information. Questions?
