Billing Insurance Well-Being About Secondary Menu Primary Care Acute Care Sports Medicine Specialty Care Lab and X-ray LGBTQIA+ Services We strive to create a welcoming space for all students who identify as LGBTQIA+. University Health Services provides confidential, inclusive, and culturally competent care for all your health care needs, and we make every effort to provide inclusive sexual and reproductive healthcare services, easy access to mental health services, and support for all forms of gender identity and expression. Note that some of our health services staff have completed the UO Queer Ally Coalition training. Go to our Team page and look for staff with the rainbow webfoot icon: Please find more resources below and we look forward to assisting you with your healthcare needs. LGBT Education and Support Services Find information and resources about UO student organizations and leadership, coming out support, calendar of events on campus, and more. UHS Counseling Services Trans and Gender-Diverse Support Services UHS Counseling Services provides comprehensive mental health and testing services to UO students. Counseling Services strives to be an affirming and supportive space for lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer, questioning, intersex (LGBTQQI) and other students who identify along the spectra of gender and sexual diversities. HIV Alliance HIV Alliance supports individuals living with HIV/AIDS and helps the community prevent new HIV infections. HIV Alliance provides support, advocacy, referral services, and emergency financial assistance to people living with HIV/AIDS.
