These sessions are typically an hour long and free to all students. Check out our calendar of events below, as well as an archive of past workshops on YouTube . Attend a Workshop Request a Workshop In addition to our scheduled events, we are happy to host a workshop for your class, organization, or event. This is a free service. We strive to make our financial wellness content as accessible as possible. You can request a workshop by submitting our request form or emailing us at financialwellness@uoregon.edu .
