Financial Eligibility: Family taxable income may not exceed 150% of the federally defined poverty line . First-Generation Eligibility: Neither parent (natural or adoptive) earned a bachelor's degree before your 18th birthday. Or you reside with and also receive support from only one parent who did not earn a bachelor's degree before you were 18. Underrepresented Eligibility: Member of a racial and/or ethnic group that is historically underrepresented in higher education; Hispanic or Latino/a/x, American Indian or Alaska Native, Asian, Black or African American, Middle Eastern or Arab, Native Hawaiian or Other Pacific Islander. Unaccompanied Youth Eligibility: Not living with family, In state custody, foster care, or homeless.
