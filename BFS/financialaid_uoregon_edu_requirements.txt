Your financial aid cannot be released until you have satisfied all outstanding requirements. Follow these steps to review and complete your financial aid requirements: 1.       Log in to DuckWeb . 2.       Click the “Financial Aid Menu” Tab 3.       Click “Eligibility and Requirements.” 4.       Select the appropriate aid year. 5.       Review Requirements. Each requirement can be clicked and the link will direct you to the instructions or form necessary to satisfy the requirement.
