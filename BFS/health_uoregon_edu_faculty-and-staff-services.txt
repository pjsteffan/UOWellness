Billing Insurance Well-Being About Secondary Menu Primary Care Acute Care Sports Medicine Specialty Care Lab and X-ray Faculty and Staff Services While UHS services are available to and prioritized for UO students, select UHS medical and ancillary services are available for UO employees. Note that visits to UHS Counseling Services are funded by the Student Administrative Health Fee (SAHF), and therefore availability of Counseling Services is generally limited to students who have paid the SAHF. Pharmacy Individuals Need a prescription refill, a spare set of tweezers, or a small package of Kleenex for your workstation? Look no further than the University Health Services Pharmacy. Current employees are able to fill prescriptions as well as purchase over-the-counter (OTC) products from the pharmacy. Pharmacists are available to assist you with finding the right product. Payment may be made with cash, check, or credit and debit cards. OTC Items for University Departments University departments can order OTC items through the pharmacy for use by their staff. We are able to charge index or account codes and are happy to send products via campus mail directly to your department. Flu Shots Drop-in flu shots are available for faculty members and staff most days at our pharmacy. Be sure to bring your insurance card, in addition to your UO ID. Call 541-346-4454 for more information. Physical Therapy and Acupuncture Faculty and staff members can access acupuncture and physical therapy services at UHS. We are not available for the treatment of Worker’s Compensation injuries at this time. Call 541-346-4401 to schedule an appointment. Durable Medical Equipment Discounted durable medical equipment (braces, arch supports, etc.) is available for purchase (cash or check) at the Physical Therapy Clinic . Dental Services The dental clinic offers a wide range of preventive and diagnostic services. Call 541-346-2039 with inquiries or to schedule an appointment.
