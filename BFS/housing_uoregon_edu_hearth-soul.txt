Sit around our open hearth oven and choose from deep dish pizzas, fresh-baked focaccia sandwiches, hand-tossed salads, and scratch-made soups. With the perfect perch overlooking the PNW Public Market, this is many students’ go-to for lunch or dinner. Hearth & Soul is located in Unthank Hall . All University Housing dining venues are open to students, faculty, staff, and community members. Meal plans , cash, Ducks Dine On , credit cards, and Duck Bucks are accepted.
