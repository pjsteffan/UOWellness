Apply for a Graduate PLUS Loan: Go to StudentAid.gov Graduate PLUS application page (secure site) Sign in using your FSA ID and password and apply If you are a first-time borrower, complete the Master Promissory Note (MPN) and entrance counseling FAFSA (Free Application for Federal Student Aid) Required Apply Online Required Three term maximum Cost of attendance minus other aid Interest rate Fixed at 8.05% (for loans first disbursed on or after July 1, 2023, and before July 1, 2024) Interest rate Fixed at 9.08% (for loans first disbursed on or after July 1, 2024, and before July 1, 2025) Net fee per disbursement 4.228% (for loans first disbursed on or after October 1, 2020, and before October 1, 2024) Grace period None Entrance counseling Required Master Promissory Note (MPN) Required If you plan to stop attending the University of Oregon at least half time in an eligible program (whether due to graduating or a change in your enrollment status), read about loan repayment and related topics including exit counseling, grace periods, loan forgiveness, and consolidation.
