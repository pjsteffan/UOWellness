All teaching and research faculty are welcome to apply. The EI is also interested in joint proposals from two or more faculty from different disciplines. Given the potential availability for more than one fellowship in the coming academic year, we encourage team proposals that connect faculty across disciplines. To apply for consideration, please submit a current CV, a short statement of interest, and two nomination letters – one from your dean and another from your department head – to UOEI@uoregon.edu . Applications will be considered on a rolling basis through the final deadline of Monday, May 1, 2023. Your statement of interest should include: How does your current research align with the EI Guiding Principles ? Does your recent or current research have external funding? What project or type of project would you propose to benefit the EI?
