Billing Insurance Well-Being About Secondary Menu After-Hours Care Self-Care Education Medical Records Medical Records Your confidentiality is a priority at University Health Services. We are committed to upholding all legal and professional obligations to protect the confidentiality of your health records. Confidentiality is central to the effective provisions of healthcare services. A confidential medical record is established and maintained for every patient who receives care at University Health Services. See our Notice of Privacy Practices for a complete description of how your health records may be used or disclosed and how you can get access to your records. To obtain a copy of your medical records, you will need to complete a release of information form. You can either complete it online through the myUOHealth student portal under the "Forms" tab, or you can download the form , complete it, and submit it through myUOHealth or bring a paper copy to the front desk of health services. Assistance with this form is available Monday through Friday from 9:00 a.m. to 5:00 p.m. Please allow 10 business days for the processing of your request.
