The hearing is titled “Building Back Better: Building Resilience for the Economy, Climate and Ecosystems.” This is the subcommittee’s first hearing of the new Congress. Watch her testimony below. “As we grapple with these new realities we now need more additional research that is interdisciplinary in nature and focused on and applied by physical science, as well as increased investigations in social science. We need more information and knowledge about both how to create climate-ready restoration projects and enhance the adaptive capacity of our communities.” - Cass Moseley UO ENVIRONMENT Faculty Excellence News Events About Report a Concern Nondiscrimination and Title IX Accessibility Privacy Policy Careers About Find People © University of Oregon .
