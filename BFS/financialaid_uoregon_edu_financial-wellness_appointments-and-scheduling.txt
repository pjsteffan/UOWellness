They are equipped to discuss a range of topics regarding your finances, and have a wealth of information to offer based in research and their own personal experiences. They are available individual advising Monday-Friday, so schedule an appointment today. Schedule a Virtual Coaching Appointment Workshops Every term we offer rotating workshop series focusing on financial literacy topics such as budgeting, credit management, studying abroad, and loan repayment. These sessions are typically an hour long and free to all students.
