With a mixture of interactive large and small group activities and guest speakers, this course will teach students how to successfully transition to the U of O and find their place looking through three lenses: academic, career, and social/interpersonal. On top of this, students will get to know a class full of fellow transfer students! Join the Transfer Student Seminar! Meet your Seminar Assistant and Instructor!
