Billing Insurance Well-Being About Secondary Menu FAQs What My Fee Pays For Get Involved Events Forms Our Team Our Mission myUOHealth Mission, Core Values, Strategic Priorities, and Vision The mission, core values, strategic priorities, and vision of University Health Services help the UO community SOAR! UHS Mission UHS collaborates with each other and the UO community to create an environment that meets people where they are, as they are, and how they are, and helps individuals become their best selves. We do this with our core values of… Caring for each other, Trusting in each other, Listening to each other, Learning from each other, and Evolving together. UHS demonstrates our commitment to this mission through our strategic priorities : With a commitment to diversity, equity, inclusion, social justice, and belonging, all through action Facilitating measurable improvements to physical and mental well-being By promoting health equity including access to services Engaging broadly with the UO community By providing outstanding health care Through the fostering of a healthy and thriving work culture Our vision is to engage with the UO community and help each other SOAR!
