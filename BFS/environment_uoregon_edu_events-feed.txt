Thu November 9, 2023 Oregon Culture Night: Traditional Weaving of the First People of Southern Oregon Thursday, November 9, 6:00-7:00 pm, Museum of Natural and Cultural History, 1680 E 15th Avenue, Eugene, OR

Seventh-generation Indigenous basketweaver Stephanie Craig (Confederated Tribes of Grand Ronde) and Dakota Zimmer (Confederated Tribes of Grand Ronde) demonstrate and discuss stick and root baby baskets from the Rogue River and Umpqua region of southern Oregon.

Included with regular admission; free for MNCH members and UO ID card holders. Show your Oregon Trail or other EBT card for an admission discount.
