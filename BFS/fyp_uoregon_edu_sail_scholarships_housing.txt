Instructions: To apply, you must meet the above qualifications and submit application materials via email, or hard copy, by May 31, 2024 : SAIL Housing scholarship application Pathway recipient letter (can be emailed once received) Send in UO email once it has been assigned A 250-word essay describing how a campus housing scholarship will enhance your academic goals and include how this scholarship will increase your ability to contribute to the university campus community If you need accessibility assistance to apply, email sailstaff@uoregon.edu .
