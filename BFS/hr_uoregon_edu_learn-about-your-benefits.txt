Programs & Services Recruitment Breadcrumb Home Learn About Your Benefits This page provides highlights of trainings, help sessions, web pages, and other resources designed to help you better understand and make the most of the benefits available to you as a UO employee. If you're looking to learn more about health insurance options, retirement savings plans, or other perks available to you, these offerings are designed to provide you with the information you need to make informed decisions about your benefits. Most of the offerings listed here are applicable to Public Employees Benefit Board (PEBB)-eligible faculty, classified staff, and officers of administration only. Jump to opportunities to learn about: General benefits and new employee information Medical, dental, and vision coverage Retirement options Wellness, services, and discounts benefits General Benefits & New Employee Education New Hire Benefit Drop-In Help Sessions (for new employees) Offered once per academic term Register in MyTrack . Benefits coordinators will be available to answer questions regarding benefit elections, how to enroll in benefits, retirement, wellness, and other university benefit offerings. Please register; arrive any time during the session for drop-in Q&A. Employee Benefits Overview Offered monthly on Zoom Register in MyTrack We’ll discuss benefit eligibility, provide a review of medical plans, and offer retirement information. We'll also discuss the menu of lesser-known benefits such as product and services discounts, care provider networks, pet insurance, and more. If you’re interested in expanding your knowledge of your benefits, please register today. Work-Life Resources for UO Employees This self-guided module provides an overview of programs that support work-life balance for University of Oregon employees. You will learn about resources for families and caregivers, wellness programs for employees, services offered by the Employee Assistance Program (EAP), and protected leave and flexible work arrangements. Other Resources Find more learning opportunities for new employees on the New Employee Orientation Programs page . How to Read and Understand Your Earnings Statement - self-guided module that explains how to access and understand the details of your UO earnings statement (paystub). You will learn about resources for families and caregivers, wellness programs for employees, services offered by the Employee Assistance Program (EAP), and protected leave and flexible work arrangements.
