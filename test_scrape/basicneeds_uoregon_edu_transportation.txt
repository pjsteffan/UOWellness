Phone: 541-346-7433 duckrides.uoregon.edu UO-only resource UO Bike Program The UO Bike Program offers a do-it-yourself maintenance shop, campus fix-it repair stations, maintenance classes, and more that are free to students. Location: EMU 008 Phone: 541-346-6092 outdoorprogram.uoregon.edu/bikeprogram UO-only resource Lane Transit District Students can use the Umo Mobility App or a TouchPass card to ride the bus for free. LTD’s website can help you plan your trip, see which routes serve UO Station, and identify park-and-ride locations. Location: 3500 East 17th Avenue, Eugene, OR 97403 Phone: 541-687-5555 www.ltd.org Community resource PeaceHealth Rides Bike Share PeaceHealth Rides allows you to rent a bike for short trips at low prices. You can pick up and return bikes from one of several locations throughout Eugene (or park them anywhere for a small fee). UO students, faculty members, and staff are eligible for discounted rates, and you may be able to ride for free if you participate in certain state or federal assistance programs. Phone: 541-214-2212 (for urgent matters) www.peacehealthrides.com Community resource Get There Oregon Get There Oregon allows individuals to join or offer carpool and vanpool opportunities. Sign up to find regular carpool partners (using the Trip Options tool) or one-time rides (using the Rideboard). Phone: 971-202-9758 getthereoregon.org Community resource Commute with Enterprise (Vanpool) A group of 5–15 commuters can join or establish a vanpool. Vanpools work best for those who live 10 miles or more from their workplace/school. Fares are based on the average monthly miles driven, the cost of the vanpool lease, and fuel expenses. www.commutewithenterprise.com Community resource RideSource Origin-to-destination transportation for people who are unable to ride the bus due to a disability. An assessment will be done to determine the services you are eligible for, but once approved, RideSource users can then call to schedule trips. Location: 3500 East 17th Avenue, Eugene, OR 97403 Phone: 541-687-5555 www.ltd.org/ridesource Community resource Zipcar Book a car by the hour or day. Prices are discounted for UO students; see the Zipcar website for current prices. Apply online, and you will be sent a Zipcard in the mail, allowing you to access Zipcars in the area. www.zipcar.com/universities/university-of-oregon Community resource Walking and Biking Find additional resources from the City of Eugene for getting around by bike and on foot, like bike maps and safety information.
