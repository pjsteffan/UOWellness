It’s your place to study, eat, and come together as a community. The EMU is also home to 250+ student groups, diverse cultural events, and everything else that makes the UO such a vibrant campus. With—and within—the EMU, you can challenge yourself: learn to blow glass at the Craft Center , sign up for a trip with Outdoor Programs , and grow as a leader in the Holden Center for Leadership and Community Engagement . There is also a host of services to help you succeed academically, including computer labs, copy center, ticket office, connections to affordable childcare, and lots of places to study. Come connect at the EMU.
