Facilities About Secondary Menu Court Reservations Facility Rentals Rental Gallery Locker and Equipment Rentals Facility Hours Map Locker and Equipment Rentals Lockers The Rec Center encourages patrons to store their belongings securely in a locker while utilizing Rec Center facilities. Day-Use Lockers Day use lockers are available in multiple locations across the Student Recreation Center including inside the locker rooms, by the Midtown Courts , between the Fitness Alley and Midtown Fitness , outside the Cycling Studio , and more. There are also open cubbies to store non-valuable items under the stairs in Midtown Fitness. Day-use locks can be checked out at Equipment Issue , and should be returned by closing on the day issued. Locker Rentals You can also rent lockers by the term or annually. These lockers are located inside the locker rooms, and outside private bathrooms and changing areas. Visit the Welcome Desk in the main lobby to rent a locker. Size Term Fee Annual Fee* 1/3 size $25 $100 1/2 size $30 $120 *Annual membership only available for purchase starting fall term. Locker Policies Lockers are available for purchase on a first-come, first-served basis. Please clean out your locker and return your lock to the Rec Center Welcome Desk by the given clearance day. Failure to do so will result in a clearance fee of $20. Items left in expired lockers will be held for 30 days and then disposed of. PE and Rec is not responsible for content left in expired lockers, or lost or stolen items. After the term of service has started, there are no locker refunds or exchanges. For every day the equipment remains unreturned, you will be assessed a $5 late fee for each outstanding item. This includes equipment found abandoned in any of our facilities. If an item is not returned within five days, the item’s full replacement value will be charged to your UO account. We will attempt to inform you of any outstanding equipment by sending an email to your official UO address. You are responsible for damaged equipment, but we will only charge replacement fees if we determine misuse. Cost of attendance is the estimated cost to attend the UO for the academic year. The Office of Student Financial Aid and Scholarships provides estimates for cost of attendance information that goes beyond tuition and fees, and includes: living expenses (housing and food), books/supplies/equipment, transportation, and miscellaneous personal expenses.
