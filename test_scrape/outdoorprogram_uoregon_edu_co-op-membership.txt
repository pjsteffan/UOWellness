If you are not a UO student, you can access the Rental Barn, the Bike Program, and a variety of events by becoming a community member. Register and pay at the Outdoor and Bike Program office in the EMU. New and renewing community members will need to purchase a one-time UO Community Card from the UO Card Office in addition to the Outdoor Program membership. The UO Card Office is located at the bottom floor of the EMU, and the card costs $10. Annual Membership Costs UO students free UO faculty members and staff $20 Non-UO affiliated community members $25 *Faculty members, staff, and others in the community can utilize the Outdoor Program without a membership for a day fee of $5.
