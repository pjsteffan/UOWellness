ALL SESSIONS FULL Rising 6th–8th About Esports Arena This program is tailored for middle school campers who are eager to immerse themselves in the world of competitive video gaming (esports) during their summer break. Open to all skill levels, this camp provides an opportunity for enthusiasts to enhance their proficiency in various esports games, fostering both personal growth and self-confidence with age-appropriate guardrails in place. Through focused training, participants will sharpen their gaming strategies and skills while also learning the importance of teamwork in a collaborative, team-oriented setting. After refining their gaming abilities, campers will engage in organized esports matches and mini-tournaments during the morning sessions. In the afternoons, campers will participate in camp games and have the opportunity to swim. Who can attend? Open to all children who will be entering sixth through eighth grade in the fall. When is the camp and how long does it last? Esports Arena takes place during the summer months. Each session lasts five days, unless otherwise specified. Drop-off for all campers in all camps will be from 7:45–9:00 a.m.; pick-up will be from 4:00–5:30 p.m. What activities will take place? Activities offered vary based on session and availability. Tutorials In-house tournaments in games like League of Legends , Super Smash Bros , and Rocket League Swimming Camp games Special events What if my preferred session is full? Families can add their campers directly to the waitlists. Please proceed to the online registration link below, log in with your account, and all full sessions will have a "Waitlist" button.
