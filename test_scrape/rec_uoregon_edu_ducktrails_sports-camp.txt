all types of sporting endeavors, our Sports Camp in Eugene is the program for them! Campers will be active and engaged while learning sports skills and strategy in this non-competitive program. Coaches and counselors will facilitate activities in a positive environment by emphasizing teamwork and good sportsmanship amongst campers. Mornings will be spent introducing campers to both traditional and non-traditional sports, and afternoons are comprised of lunchtime and free-choice activities. Who can attend? Open to all children who will be entering second through fifth grade in the fall. When is the camp and how long does it last? Sports Camps take place during the summer months. Each session lasts five days, unless otherwise specified.* Drop-off for all campers in all camps will be from 7:45–9:00 a.m.; pick-up will be from 4:00–5:30 p.m. * In Session 2 there will be no camp on July 4 and 5, in observance of Independence Day. What activities will take place? Campers will learn sports skills in a non-competitive environment. Various sports Swimming Free-choice activities What if my preferred session is full? Families can add their campers directly to the waitlists. Please proceed to the online registration link below, log in with your account, and all full sessions will have a "Waitlist" button.
