It includes: Listing and resources sites UO housing Property management companies Nonprofit housing providers Apartment complexes You can use the links in the “Listing and Resources Sites” sections to find current listings for both large and small properties. Contacting a housing provider directly (website, phone, email, or in-person visit) can be a valuable way to learn about the provider and find openings that may or may not be listed publicly. The property management organizations and individual properties included below each provide housing to more than fifteen students and operate properties within two miles of the UO’s Eugene campus.
