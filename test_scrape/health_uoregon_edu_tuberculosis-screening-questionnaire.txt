Billing Insurance Well-Being About Secondary Menu Immunization Requirements TB Risk Assessment Insurance Requirements Tuberculosis Risk Assessment All incoming students must complete a tuberculosis (TB) risk assessment. The TB risk assessment is located on the myUOHealth portal under the “Forms” tab. Incoming students who are assessed as potentially at higher risk for TB infection will be required to undergo further evaluation and testing. TB testing performed by a provider other than University Health Services must be completed no earlier than six months prior to arrival on campus. Submitting Documentation Complete your TB risk assessment online through the myUOHealth portal. To log in to myUOHealth: User ID is your Duck ID—the University of Oregon email address. Password is your email password. Complete the TB risk assessment under the "Forms" tab. If you are unable to access the student portal, you may submit your TB risk assessment by mail, email, or fax.
