Fitness Classes Make friends when joining one of our group fitness classes like yoga, Zumba, or bootcamp and learn from trained instructors. Explore Fitness Classes Personal Training For more personalized instruction, sign up for Personal Training and create a customized workout plan. Explore Personal Training Intramural Sports Grab some friends to play in Intramural Sports leagues and tournaments like basketball, flag football, and volleyball at the competitive or recreational level. Explore Intramural Sports Aquatics Make a splash by swimming laps, diving in the deep end, or playing water volleyball in the pool. Or, just relax in the hot tub. Lifeguards are on duty to ensure your safety. Explore Aquatics Rock District Climb to new heights. When scaling the boulders and climbing walls in the Rock District, you'll have people supporting you. Gear is provided. Explore the Rock District Cost of attendance is the estimated cost to attend the UO for the academic year. The Office of Student Financial Aid and Scholarships provides estimates for cost of attendance information that goes beyond tuition and fees, and includes: living expenses (housing and food), books/supplies/equipment, transportation, and miscellaneous personal expenses.
