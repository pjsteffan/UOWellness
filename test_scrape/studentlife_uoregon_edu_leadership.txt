Try running for ASUO Senate, organizing an ice hockey team, or coordinating the sexual violence prevention workshop for your residence hall. There are formal leadership opportunities through the Holden Center for Leadership and Community Engagement and through the many student jobs in departments all over campus. Engage can help you find other opportunities as well. Are you thinking about that perennial question: “What are you going to do after college?” We can help! The University Career Center has dozens of workshops, special events with employers visiting campus, and one-on-one career coaching to help you achieve your long-term career goals.
