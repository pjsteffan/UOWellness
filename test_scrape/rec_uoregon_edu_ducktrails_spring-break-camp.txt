About Secondary Menu Duck Trails Camps Family Recreation Lessons Spring Break Camp Rising Kindergartners–5th About Spring Break Camp This Spring Break Camp includes three days of Duck Trails Classic Camp programming. Campers will swim, rock climb, play sports, enjoy camp games, and participate in a variety of other activities as we use the Student Recreation Center as our playground! Who can attend? Open to all children between five and eleven years old. When is the camp and how long does it last? The next Spring Break Camp will take place March 25–27, 2024, from 8:30 a.m. to 4:30 p.m. each day. Registration will open at 1:00 p.m. on Friday, February 23, for both online and in person. What activities will take place? This camp includes the same programming as the Classic Duck Trails Camp. Swimming Rock climbing Various sports Camp games Registration You can register on RecWeb or register in person at the Rec Service Center . Register for Spring Break Camp Registration Information What if my preferred session is full? To be added to the waitlist, please email ducktrails@uoregon.edu with the camp(s) and session(s) you want, as well as your child's name, birthdate, and an emergency contact phone number. Cost of attendance is the estimated cost to attend the UO for the academic year.
