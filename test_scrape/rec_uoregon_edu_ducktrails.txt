Duck Trails staff create a low pressure, high energy, positive environment where kids are encouraged to build friendships and explore their creativity and sense of identity. Each of our Eugene summer and school year camps feature a variety of unique activities designed to promote teamwork and friendship, and help children grow their self-esteem, confidence, leadership, and social skills. School-Year Camps at the University of Oregon Stay active and engaged during the school year! These camps take place during breaks in the academic year, including no-school days, winter break, and spring break. They are a shortened version of our summer camp programming.
