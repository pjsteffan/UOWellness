The Basic Needs Program has funds available to assist students experiencing financial insecurity with purchasing winter clothing and accessories. Eligible students can receive a $75 Duck Store gift card. Funds are available on a first-come, first-served basis. The online application will close when all the funds have been exhausted. Approved students will be notified to pick up their gift card from the Office of the Dean of Students during open hours (8:00–11:00 a.m. and 1:00–5:00 p.m. weekdays). If you are unable to get your card before December 8, your card will be available for pick up during week 1 of winter term, beginning on Monday, January 8, 2024.
