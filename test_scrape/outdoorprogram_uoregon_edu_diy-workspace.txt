The workspace has work stands, tools, and “how-to” maintenance books. Bike Program staff are on hand to help you navigate the space. Faculty, staff, alumni, and community members can use the workspace with a co-op membership . Non-members can purchase a day pass for $5.00. Contact the Bike Program Monday–Friday | noon–5:00 p.m. 541-346-6092 Email Location Repair Support We offer a series of hands-on classes designed to teach you how to make small modifications such as changing a flat tire or adjusting your brakes as well as large-scale repairs. View Events Retail and Parts We sell limited retail parts including tubes, lights, and locks. If you need a part beyond what we sell to repair your bike, please visit a local bike shop first. Once you obtain the parts, you can use the tools in the DIY workspace to install the part.
