February 2023 is the last month emergency food benefits will be distributed. Students receiving SNAP will continue to get their regular SNAP benefit amount in March and every month after March. Students can check their regular SNAP food benefit amount by logging into their ONE account or using the EBT Edge app. The external information and links on this website have been compiled to assist students and others in navigating and securing services and benefits external to the university. Eligibility for any benefit or service is determined by the agency or organization responsible for the benefit or service. The links are provided for information and do not represent an endorsement by the University of Oregon.
