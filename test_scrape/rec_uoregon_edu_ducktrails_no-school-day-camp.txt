About Secondary Menu Duck Trails Camps Family Recreation Lessons No-School Day Camp Rising Kindergartners–5th About No-School Day Camps Give your child something to do on non-school days in Eugene with these Duck Trails day camps. Children will learn through fun activities while making new friends and gaining confidence. Who can attend? Open to all children between five and eleven years old. When is the camp and how long does it last? A one-day camp during the spring will be held on Friday, June 7, 2024. The session will be from 8:30 a.m.–4:30 p.m. No before or after care will be offered. What activities will take place? The programming of this camp will be the same as the classic camp. Kids will participate in activities throughout the Rec Center including: Swimming Arts and crafts Rock climbing (Fridays only) Global kids Camp games Sports Special events Registration You can register on RecWeb or register in person at the Welcome Desk . Register for No-School Day Camp Registration Information What if my preferred session is full? To be added to the waitlist, please email ducktrails@uoregon.edu with the camp(s) and session(s) you want, as well as your child's name, birthdate, and an emergency contact phone number. Cost of attendance is the estimated cost to attend the UO for the academic year.
